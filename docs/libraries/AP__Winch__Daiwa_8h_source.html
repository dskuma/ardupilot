16/*
17 The Daiwa winch is produced by a Japanese company called Okaya.
18 There are two PWM controls supported:
19 - the rate control for releasing (high PWM) or retracting (low PWM) the line
20 - the clutch control has three settings:
21 - released (high PWM) lets the winch spin freely
22 - engaged soft (middle PWM) allows the rate control to work but it may slip
23 if too much tension is required. This driver does not use this setting.
24 - engaged hard (low PWM) allows the rate control to work regardless of tension.
25 A telemetry output from the winch is connected to the autopilot and provides
26 the amount of line released, tension, clutch setting, etc.
27*/
