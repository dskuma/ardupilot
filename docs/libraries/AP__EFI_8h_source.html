31/*
32 * This library aims to read data from Electronic Fuel Injection
33 * or Engine Control units. It is focused around the generic
34 * internal combustion engine state message provided by the
35 * UAVCAN protocol due to its comprehensiveness, but is extensible
36 * to use other forms of data transfer besides UAVCAN.
37 *
38 *
39 *
40 * Authors: Sriram Sami and David Ingraham
41 * With direction from Andrew Tridgell, Robert Lefebvre, Francisco Ferreira and
42 * Pavel Kirienko.
43 * Thanks to Yonah, SpektreWorks Inc, and HFE International.
44 */
