6/*
7 * Pivot Turn controller for skid-steering rovers and boats
8 *
9 * How-to-Use:
10 * 1. call "enable(true)" once for skid-steering vehicles to enable this controller
11 * 2. before vehicle starts towards a waypoint call "check_activation" and provide the earth-frame heading to the waypoint
12 * this may change the controller's internal state to "active"
13 * 3. on each main loop iteration call "active()" to see if this controller thinks it is controllering the vehicle
14 * 4. call "get_turn_rate_rads()" to retrieve the desired turn rate towards the next waypoint
15 * 5. pass above turn rate into the rate controller, set speed controller's speed to zero
16 * 6. this controller's "active" state will change to false once it has completed the pivot turn
17 */
