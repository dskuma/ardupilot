203 /*
204 * Write body frame linear and angular displacement measurements from a visual odometry sensor
205 *
206 * quality is a normalised confidence value from 0 to 100
207 * delPos is the XYZ change in linear position measured in body frame and relative to the inertial reference at timeStamp_ms (m)
208 * delAng is the XYZ angular rotation measured in body frame and relative to the inertial reference at timeStamp_ms (rad)
209 * delTime is the time interval for the measurement of delPos and delAng (sec)
210 * timeStamp_ms is the timestamp of the last image used to calculate delPos and delAng (msec)
211 * delay_ms is the average delay of external nav system measurements relative to inertial measurements
212 * posOffset is the XYZ body frame position of the camera focal point (m)
213 */

216 /*
217 * Write odometry data from a wheel encoder. The axis of rotation is assumed to be parallel to the vehicle body axis
218 *
219 * delAng is the measured change in angular position from the previous measurement where a positive rotation is produced by forward motion of the vehicle (rad)
220 * delTime is the time interval for the measurement of delAng (sec)
221 * timeStamp_ms is the time when the rotation was last measured (msec)
222 * posOffset is the XYZ body frame position of the wheel hub (m)
223 * radius is the effective rolling radius of the wheel (m)
224 * this should not be called at more than the EKF's update rate (50hz or 100hz)

228 /*
229 * Writes the measurement from a yaw angle sensor
230 *
231 * yawAngle: Yaw angle of the vehicle relative to true north in radians where a positive angle is
232 * produced by a RH rotation about the Z body axis. The Yaw rotation is the first rotation in a
233 * 321 (ZYX) or a 312 (ZXY) rotation sequence as specified by the 'type' argument.
234 * yawAngleErr is the 1SD accuracy of the yaw angle measurement in radians.
235 * timeStamp_ms: System time in msec when the yaw measurement was taken. This time stamp must include
236 * all measurement lag and transmission delays.
237 * type: An integer specifying Euler rotation order used to define the yaw angle.
238 * type = 1 specifies a 312 (ZXY) rotation order, type = 2 specifies a 321 (ZYX) rotation order.
